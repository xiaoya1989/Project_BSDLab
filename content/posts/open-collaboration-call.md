我们欢迎对脑状态动力学、意识与感知、神经振荡以及脑机接口感兴趣的学生和合作伙伴加入 BSD Lab。

实验室特别欢迎具有心理学、神经科学、工程、计算或交叉学科背景的同学。即使你并不具备所有方向的经验，只要对跨学科研究保持好奇并愿意投入，我们都非常欢迎你联系。

如果你希望进一步了解实验室研究与加入方式，欢迎通过网站与我们取得联系。

<!-- en -->
We welcome students and collaborators interested in brain-state dynamics, consciousness and perception, neural oscillations, and brain-computer interfaces to join BSD Lab.

We especially encourage applicants with backgrounds in psychology, neuroscience, engineering, computation, or related interdisciplinary fields. You do not need expertise in every area; curiosity and the willingness to work across disciplines matter most.

If you would like to learn more about the lab and possible opportunities, please reach out through the website.
