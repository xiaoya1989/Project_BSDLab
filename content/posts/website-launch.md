我们正式发布了 Brain State Dynamics Lab（BSD Lab）官方网站的首个公开版本。

这一版本将作为实验室对外展示的主要入口，集中介绍实验室的研究方向、团队成员、论文发表与加入方式，也会在后续持续更新实验室新闻、项目进展和招生信息。

未来，网站将与微信公众号内容逐步联动，形成统一的实验室内容发布渠道。

<!-- en -->
We have launched the first public version of the Brain State Dynamics Lab (BSD Lab) website.

This initial release serves as the lab's main public-facing entry point, introducing our research directions, team, publications, and opportunities to join. It will also become the place where we share lab news, project milestones, and recruitment updates.

In the next phase, the website will be connected with our WeChat Official Account so that both channels can share a unified publishing workflow.
