近期，BSD Lab 组织了一次轻松愉快的团建活动，也借这个机会开展了新一期的 Method Club。

在这次分享中，韩彪老师围绕**脑电数据预处理（EEG preprocessing）**做了系统介绍，带大家一起梳理了从原始信号到可分析数据之间的关键步骤，包括数据清理、伪迹处理、参考方式选择，以及后续分析前需要特别注意的方法细节。

Method Club 一直是实验室内部很重要的交流形式。除了帮助大家建立对经典分析流程的整体理解，也为之后开展更规范、更可复现的脑电研究打下基础。

这次活动也让大家在正式讨论之外，有机会用更轻松的方式交流想法、分享经验，进一步增强实验室的凝聚力。

![](/posts/method-club/method-club2.jpg)

我们也期待后续继续围绕神经振荡、脑状态动力学、感知与脑机接口等主题开展更多内部学习和交流活动。

<!-- en -->
BSD Lab recently held a relaxed team gathering together with a new Method Club session.

In this session, Biao Han gave a focused introduction to **EEG preprocessing**, walking the group through key steps between raw recordings and analysis-ready data, including data cleaning, artifact handling, referencing strategies, and methodological choices that matter before downstream analyses.

Method Club has become an important format for internal exchange in the lab. It helps us build a shared understanding of core analytical workflows and lays the groundwork for more rigorous and reproducible EEG research in future projects.

Beyond the formal discussion, the gathering also gave everyone more time to talk, exchange ideas, and strengthen the lab community in a relaxed setting.

![](/posts/method-club/method-club2.jpg)

We look forward to holding more internal sessions around neural oscillations, brain-state dynamics, perception, and brain-computer interfaces in the near future.
