<!-- zh -->
实验室启动了新的跨模态脑状态动力学研究计划。

该计划将围绕振荡动力学、知觉推断和多感觉整合展开，连接 EEG、fMRI 与人颅内记录等不同层级的数据分析路径，进一步理解大尺度脑网络中的信息流动。

这项计划也将为后续闭环调控研究提供理论与方法基础。

<!-- en -->
The lab has started a new cross-modal brain-state dynamics program.

This initiative links oscillatory dynamics, perceptual inference, and multisensory integration across EEG, fMRI, and human intracranial recordings to better understand information flow in large-scale brain networks.

It also provides the conceptual and methodological basis for our future closed-loop intervention work.
