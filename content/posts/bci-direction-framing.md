实验室已经正式启动闭环脑机接口研究方向。

这一方向将围绕脑状态的实时追踪、动态解码以及与行为结果相联系的自适应刺激展开，重点探索如何从“观察脑活动”进一步走向“与脑活动实时交互”。

这也将成为 BSD Lab 后续长期发展的重要主线之一。

<!-- en -->
The lab has officially initiated its closed-loop brain-computer interface research direction.

This program focuses on real-time brain-state tracking, dynamic decoding, and adaptive stimulation linked to behavior, with the goal of moving from observing brain activity to interacting with it in real time.

It will be one of the long-term core directions of BSD Lab.
